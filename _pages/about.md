I'm a senior Computer Science student at Bilkent University and an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin. At UT Austin, I will be advised by [Dr. Jovan Stojkovic](https://jovan-s.github.io/) in the CASCADE Lab. My research interests are in the field of computer architecture and systems for AI.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on **computer architecture and systems for AI**, broadly in these areas:

1. Computer architecture and hardware-software co-design.
2. Systems for AI and machine learning workloads.
3. Hardware acceleration for large-scale models.
